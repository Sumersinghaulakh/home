Jack Andrews

Nexs was founded in the year 2180 by an engineer named Jack Andrews. The company's mission was to make space travel more accessible and affordable for everyone. It quickly gained popularity and grew to become one of the largest companies in the world. The company brought humanity into the stars and beyond with its advanced technologies and vision for the future. Today, the company is a driving force in the expansion of the human race throughout the galaxy and beyond. It continues to innovate and explore ways to make space travel safer, faster, and easier for everyone.
